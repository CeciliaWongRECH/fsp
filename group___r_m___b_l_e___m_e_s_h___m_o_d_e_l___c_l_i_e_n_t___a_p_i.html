BLE Mesh Model Client Interface
Interfaces

Detailed Description

Interface for Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Model Client functions.

Summary

The BLE Mesh interface for the Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Model Client (BLE MESH MODEL CLIENT) middleware provides Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Model Client functionality.

The Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Model Client interface can be implemented by:

  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Battery Client (rm_mesh_generic_battery_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Default Transition Time Client (rm_mesh_generic_dtt_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Level Client (rm_mesh_generic_level_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Location Client (rm_mesh_generic_loc_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic On Off Client (rm_mesh_generic_on_off_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Power Level Client (rm_mesh_generic_pl_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Power On Off Client (rm_mesh_generic_poo_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Generic Property Client (rm_mesh_generic_prop_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Health Client (rm_mesh_health_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Light Control Client (rm_mesh_light_ctl_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Light Hsl Client (rm_mesh_light_hsl_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Light Location Client (rm_mesh_light_lc_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Light Lightness Client (rm_mesh_light_lightness_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Light Xyl Client (rm_mesh_light_xyl_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Scene Client (rm_mesh_scene_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Scheduler Client (rm_mesh_scheduler_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Time Client (rm_mesh_time_clt)
  • Bluetooth Low Energy Mesh Network Sensor Client (rm_mesh_sensor_clt)
